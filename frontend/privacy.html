Privacy Policy

How TaskManager collects, uses, and protects your information

This Privacy Policy explains how TaskManager (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use this application.

1. Information We Collect

TaskManager is a simple task management tool. The primary information stored is the content of the tasks you create (titles, descriptions, and status). We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to include it in your task content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Store and display your tasks in the application
  • Allow you to update and delete your tasks
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the service

3. Data Storage

Task data is stored in a database (MongoDB) managed by our hosting provider(s). We take reasonable technical measures to secure this data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The current version of TaskManager does not use cookies or third‑party tracking scripts for analytics or advertising. Standard server logs may be collected by the hosting provider for security and operational purposes.

5. Third‑Party Services

We may use third‑party hosting providers (such as Render and Vercel) to serve this application and its API. These providers may process limited technical data (such as IP address and request metadata) as part of delivering the service.

6. Data Retention

Your task data is retained for as long as it is stored in the application. You can delete tasks at any time using the delete option in the interface.

7. Your Rights

If you are using this project as part of your own deployment or portfolio, you may adapt this policy to reflect your specific legal requirements. For production use, you are responsible for ensuring this policy complies with the laws applicable in your jurisdiction.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the content on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the project owner using the contact details provided in the project’s repository or portfolio.
